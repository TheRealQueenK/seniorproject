Welcome to RateNCAT!!

Hey Aggie Family! This is a site where you can rate as a Student or Faculty member to communicate and share your thoughts on the different facilities, professors, and other university-related things. Users will be able to leave a rating post that is out of five stars. In this post, the user could choose to leave just a star rating and/or comment on their rating. This will give users the ability to elaborate on how they felt and why they gave a particular rating.
